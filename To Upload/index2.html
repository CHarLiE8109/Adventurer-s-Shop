About

What is our backstory

  • 2000-2002

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Back in the day when adventuring was in its peak, trading was the main concern of our enterprise, bringing solid goods to solid customers on a timely basis.

  • 2003-2008

    Tools

    After years of experience trading goods for adventurers, we started to ask an important question? If we know what's good for adventurers, why not make really good stuff for them? Our manufacturing and tooling facility was born.

  • January 2009

    Professional Talent

    Our taste for good equipment soon caught the eye of the respected Blacksmiths and Artisans Guild. A partnership was formed, and we are proud to inform our customers that only the best guild members work on our customers' equipments and needs.

  • July 2013

    Industralisation and mass production

    What better way to warm up in the southern hemisphere's winter, than to turn the furnace on! We can now supply larger groups of adventurers, mercenaries and armies. Not only that, production time is reduced tremendously and mending works happen 3x faster. What better way to keep our adventurers adventuring!
